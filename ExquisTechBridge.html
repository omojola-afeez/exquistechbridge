What you walk away with

Built for
senior roles.

Industry-Grade Certificate
A verified digital certificate backed by real project work, not just attendance. Score 60%+ to qualify. Premium certificates (80%+) carry a detailed skills summary and direct employer introductions, carrying the weight of a portfolio review.
Production Portfolio
Eight weeks of real, evaluated project submissions, pushed to GitHub, documented, and reviewed to professional standards. By Demo Day you have a portfolio that demonstrates depth, not just familiarity. The kind that earns callbacks from senior hiring managers.
Employer Pipeline Access
Premium certificate holders are matched with hiring partners seeking talent in their track. We build your 1-page technical profile from 8 weeks of submission history: a data-driven snapshot of your actual skills, used to make warm introductions to relevant companies.
Expert Mentor Network
Live sessions with working engineers and industry professionals in your track: working professionals, not academics or bootcamp graduates. Weekly peer collaboration from Week 4. A professional network of cohort peers and mentors that outlasts the programme itself.
Simple by design

How it
works.

No classes. No schedules. Just tasks, feedback, and progress. Every single week.

01
Apply by June 5
Fill in the form below. We read every application. 5 spots per track. First-come, serious commitment prioritised. You will hear back within 48 hours.
02
Get added to your track channel
You will be added to your track's dedicated community channel (Discord or Slack). All communication, tasks, and feedback flow through there. No extra apps.
03
Complete weekly tasks and get evaluated
Each week you will receive a task matched to your track and current skill level. Submit your work via GitHub or Google Doc. Every submission is reviewed against a 5-dimension rubric (Correctness, Depth, Creativity, Documentation, and Timeliness) with detailed written feedback delivered directly to your community channel.
04
Demo Day · Week 8
Top projects per track present live on a virtual Demo Day. Employer partners attend to evaluate and hire. Top performer per track receives a merit stipend and premium certificate with direct employer introductions.
